# DrinkUp App Icon Design Brief

## Size Requirements
- **Primary:** 1024x1024px PNG (App Store)
- **No transparency:** Must have solid background
- **Safe area:** Keep important content in center 80%

---

## Design Concept

### Style: Modern, Bold, Party Vibe

**Primary Elements:**
- 🍻 Beer mugs clinking (main icon)
- Neon glow effect (matches app theme)
- Dark background (#0A0A1A or gradient)

**Color Palette:**
- Background: Dark navy/black (#0A0A1A)
- Accent 1: Electric blue (#667eea)
- Accent 2: Purple (#764ba2)
- Highlight: Neon orange/gold (#FFA500)

---

## Design Options

### Option 1: Minimalist Beer Mugs
```
[Dark circular background]
  🍻 Two beer mugs clinking
  Neon glow around mugs (blue/purple gradient)
  Simple, recognizable from small sizes
```

### Option 2: Typography + Icon
```
"DU" letters in bold, modern font
Beer mug icon integrated into the "U"
Gradient background (purple to blue)
```

### Option 3: Neon Sign Style
```
Beer mug outline in neon blue
Small sparkles/bubbles around it
Dark background with subtle gradient
Gives "nightlife" vibe
```
